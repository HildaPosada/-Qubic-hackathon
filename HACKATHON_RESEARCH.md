## üèÜ SPONSOR TECHNOLOGY: QUBIC

### What is Qubic?

Qubic is a Layer 1 blockchain that merges unmatched scalable technology with artificial general intelligence (AGI).

**Founded by:** Sergey Ivancheglo (Creator of NXT, Co-founder of IOTA)

### üöÄ Key Technical Features

#### 1. **Unprecedented Speed**
- **15.5M TPS** - Fastest blockchain ever (Certik certified)
- **55M+ transfers/second** capability
- **Instant finality** - No confirmation delays
- **Feeless transactions** - Zero gas fees

#### 2. **Smart Contracts (Turing-Complete)**
- Written in **C++** for bare-metal performance
- Direct code execution (no VM overhead)
- IPO model for smart contract launches
- Enables passive income for contract holders

#### 3. **AI Integration (Aigarth)**
- **Useful Proof of Work (UPoW)** - Mining powers AI training
- Billions of Artificial Neural Networks (ANNs)
- Draws from hundreds of thousands of miners
- Transforms computational waste into intelligence

#### 4. **Consensus & Infrastructure**
- **676 validators** called "Computors"
- Quorum-based computation
- Decentralized oracle network (in development)
- Real-world data integration for smart contracts
